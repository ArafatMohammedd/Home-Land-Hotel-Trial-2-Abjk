About Us

The Home Land Hotel first opened its doors in the last quarter of 2022, marking the beginning of a new chapter in hospitality in Adama. Our mission is simple: to provide a warm and welcoming environment where every guest feels at home.At Home Land Hotel, we believe in offering more than just a place to stay. We aim to create an experience where comfort meets convenience. Our rooms are thoughtfully designed to cater to both business and leisure travelers, featuring contemporary furnishings, high-speed internet, and serene views of the city. Whether you're here for a short visit or an extended stay, our goal is to ensure that every moment of your time with us is enjoyable and relaxing.Nestled in the heart of Adama, our hotel combines modern comfort with a touch of traditional charm. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll be greeted with the friendly smiles of our dedicated staff, ready to make your stay memorable. Our commitment to exceptional service is evident in every aspect of our hotel, from our meticulously designed rooms to our diverse range of amenities.
